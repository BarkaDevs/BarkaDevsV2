BARKADEVS - Team History

(where did it all start)

BarkaDevs started as a group of IT students at SEAIT, led by Keyan Andy Delgado. Brought together by a shared passion for web development, we decided to team up and call ourselves BarkaDevs. What began as late-night problem-solving sessions and brainstorming ideas turned into a team focused on building real-world digital solutions.

We take pride in turning code into functional, meaningful websites. Guided by Keyan, we value teamwork, innovation, and the joy of creating impactful projects.

More than just a team, BarkaDevs is a community built on shared growth and a passion for technology. We thrive on collaboration and embrace the challenges of web development, always striving to make technology work for people.

Joining BarkaDevs means being part of a story of continuous learning, creativity, and purpose-driven development.
